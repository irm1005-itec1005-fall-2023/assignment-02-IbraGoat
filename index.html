Qatar 2022 Summary

What happened in qatar 2022?

  • There were 32 teams that qualified
  • Argentina beat France in the Finals (penalty shootout)
  • Croatia placed third, followed by Morocco at fourth
  • This was Qatars first time hosting the World Cup
  • The prize money for the World Cup was $440 million
  • Qatar had the least amount of stadiums of any World Cup at 8
